Painterly Rendering

Purpose

To take an image and process it so that it appears to be painted

Design Challenges

Clipping of Paint Stroke

The Canny Edge Detection algorithm was used to compute the edges to separate regions of the image during painting. It is used to clip any paint stroke that may cross and edge.

Orientation Paint Stroke

The orientation of paint stroke was made to align with the normal to the gradient of image intensity.

Make Painting Appear Realistic

I added random pertubations to both the stroke intensity and stroke orientation so that it appears to be more realistic as an actual person would not have perfect strokes and colors on the image.

Result

The Painterly Rendering program works perfectly as shown in the Report.
