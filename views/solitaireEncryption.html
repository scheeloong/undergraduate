Solitaire Encryption

Purpose

Implement the Solitaire Encryption Algorithm using a circular linked list to model a deck of cards.

Design Challenges

Circular Linked List

Solitaire Encryption Algorithm can be performed real life with just a deck of cards. I implement this deck of cards with a circular linked list.
